Obstacles are placed throughout the map of the game randomly by the game server at the beginning of each game. A tank cannot move in to an area with an obstacle. Moreover, the tank would lose health if it tries to collide with an obstacle. There are 3 types of obstacles.

  • Brick wall
    • A tank cannot move through a brick wall.
    • A bullet cannot move through the wall but can damage it by 25% of its initial condition.

  • Stone wall
    • A tank cannot move through a stone wall.
    • A bullet cannot move through the wall and cannot damage the wall.

  • Water body
    • A tank cannot move through the water body and will result in instant death if a tank moves to an area with water.
    • A bullet will pass through the water body.
