Collecting points is the main objective of the tank game. The player with highest points will win at the end of the game. Points can be obtained mainly by,

  • Breaking bricks

    • Every bullet hit on brick walls adds 10 points to the tank which shot the bullet.

  • Collecting coins

    • Collecting coins will add points equivalent to the value of the collected coin pile.

  • Spoils of war

    • When a tank destroys another tank, the killer will get 25% of the value of the points obtained by the destroyed tank.
