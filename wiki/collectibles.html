Collectibles are placed on the game map, at random locations at random times by the game server. A tank can collect an item by moving in to the square containing the collectible.

  • Life pack
    • Tanks can collect life packs to increase the health by 20% of its initial value. Initially tanks will have 100% health and through life packs they can increase health beyond 100%.
    • Life packs have a time limit before they become disappeared automatically. Hence they must be collected before the timer expires. This information is broadcasted to all players by the game server.

  • Coin pile
    • Collecting coin piles will increase a playersâ€™ coins and points during in the game. Coin piles will appear on the map according to following ways,
      1. The game server places coin piles randomly on the map.
      2. When a tank gets destroyed, coins collected by that tank will spill on the place where it got destroyed.
    • Coin piles have a time limit before they become disappeared automatically. Hence they must be collected before the timer expires. This information is broadcasted to all players by the game server.
